'You Already Know: The Science of Mastering Your Intuition', by Laura Huang

YOU

ALREADY

KNOW

LAURA HUANG

When faced with a difficult
business decision, do you
already know what to do?
Laura Huang urges us to
pay attention to that
nagging feeling a hire
might not be quite right, or
a risky investment is the
next big thing. Core to her
approach is intentional
intuition: interrogating
uneasy feelings and
drawing on experience to
challenge why you feel
them.

The book highlights
leaders who have followed
their instincts to success,
though some are less an
endorsement for going
with your gut, than for
treating your labour force
with respect and dignity.

The book meanders into
self-help territory in
places, including exercises
and a cover evoking
Eckhart Tolle's The Power
of Now.

Ultimately, it serves as a
reminder to pay attention
to your emotions, and
avoid the grifters.
