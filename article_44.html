Apostrophes are not computers only blind spot

Re Sarah O’Connor’s discovery that she is not machine readable (“Why does the internet hate my apostrophe?’, Opinion, July 22), apostrophes are not the only problem. Computers love standardisation and hate long names. I am constantly told I have an invalid name or it is attenuated.

Lesley Victoria von Schulthess
Rechberg Preston
Cowes, Isle of Wight, UK
