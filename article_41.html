The Vatican and those ‘act of God’ contract terms

In his column “Trump — emperor of Brazil” (Opinion, July 23), Edward Luce remarked that “the Vatican is no trading entity”. While modest in scale, its trading activity is evidently material enough for the International Swaps and Derivatives Association to recently issue a netting memo on contracts entered into within Vatican jurisdiction. Such opinions typically assess the enforceability of derivatives close-out provisions in the event of default or insolvency. One must assume that appropriate theological consideration was given to any “Act of God” clauses.
